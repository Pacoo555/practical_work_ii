Overview of computer engineering

The Computer Engineering degree provides a solid foundation in hardware and software, giving students a comprehensive understanding of how computers function and interact. The program aims to equip students with the technical expertise, analytical skills, and innovative mindset to design, develop, and maintain advanced computing solutions. It also incorporates studies in ethics and critical thinking to address the societal impact of technology.
